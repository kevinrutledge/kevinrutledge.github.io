My Transition into Tech

My journey into tech wasn't a straight line. It started on the athletic track and ultimately led me to software engineering. Here's how I made the leap.

From Athlete to Coach: Building a Foundation

Early in my career, I was immersed in the world of sports, first as an athlete and then as a track and field coach. Coaching, particularly in jumps, instilled in me the importance of discipline, leadership, and teamwork — skills that have proven invaluable in the tech world.

A New Direction: Logistics and the Spark of Optimization

Seeking a more sustainable career path, I transitioned to a Warehouse Logistics Specialist role at Milestone Technologies. This experience exposed me to the critical role of precision and efficiency in inventory management, sparking my interest in process optimization through technology.
